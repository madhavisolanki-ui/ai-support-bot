CORPORATE HUMAN RESOURCES POLICY (Policy ID: HR-2026-001)
1. LEAVE & ATTENDANCE MANAGEMENT
Annual Entitlement: All full-time employees are eligible for 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year.

Request Submission:

Short-term Leave (<3 days): Please submit requests via the HR Portal at least 7 days in advance.

Long-term Leave (>3 days): Requests must be submitted at least 30 days prior to the leave start date.

Approval: Leaves are subject to team capacity and formal managerial approval.

2. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT & DRESS CODE
Standard Policy: The company observes a "Smart Casual" dress code for daily operations.

Client Engagement: Formal attire is strictly mandatory for client meetings, board presentations, and external corporate events.

3. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
Appraisal Cycle: Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually (June and December).

Compensation & Growth: Annual bonuses, salary increments, and promotion eligibility are directly linked to the outcomes of these bi-annual performance evaluations.

4. OFFICE AMENITIES & ACCESS
Cafeteria: Operational hours are from 08:00 AM to 07:00 PM daily.

Fitness Center: Access is complimentary for all employees. Entry is granted upon presentation of a valid, active Employee ID Card.

5. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL MECHANISM
We encourage an open and transparent workplace. Should an employee have a grievance:

Step 1: Initiate an informal discussion with your immediate Reporting Manager to resolve the concern.

Step 2: If the issue remains unresolved, escalate the matter formally to the HR Department at hr-support@company.com.
